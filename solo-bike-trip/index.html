About

I quit my job in Oct 2020 thinking I would do a startup. But I wanted to take a bit of time & travel too. I got through Facebook London & then I ditched my startup plan to join Facebook (pretty lame but guess that was the reality). I wanted to do a ride from Bangalore, India to London but I got frustrated when Covid forced countries to shut their land borders. That is when I decided to do a pan India solo motorbike trip.

Unfortunately I could only do it half as India was hit by 2nd Covid wave in April 2021. I was on the road for 44 days & wrote 1 post for my experience of the day. Below is the list of the blogs. All my blogs are on substack.com & clicking on any of them will take you to pocha.substack.com.
